# Step 5 — Session 4 Writing Instructions

## Prompt Task
Give the patient Session 4 specific writing instructions.

Deliver conversationally:
1. "Today, I want you to continue to write about the trauma."
2. "As with your writing in the last session, you can select
   a specific part of the trauma to write about — the part
   that was most upsetting to you."
3. "Today, I would also like you to write about how the trauma
   event has changed your life. You might write about whether
   the trauma has changed the way you view your life, the
   meaning of life, and how you relate to other people."
4. "Throughout the session I want you to really let go and
   write about your deepest thoughts and feelings."
5. "Do you have any questions?"

## Judge Criteria
pass=true when:
1. Writing instructions delivered
2. Patient acknowledged

## Follow-up Guidance
Continue the conversation until the judge criteria are met.
