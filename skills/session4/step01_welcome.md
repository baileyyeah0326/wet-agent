# Step 1 — Welcome Back

## Prompt Task
Welcome the patient back for Session 2.

1. "Welcome back, I'm glad you're here."
2. Acknowledge that coming back takes effort
3. If needed, normalize any anxiety or uncertainty
4. Ask how they're feeling about being here today

2-3 sentences.

## Judge Criteria
pass=true when:
1. Patient responded to the greeting
2. The therapist has acknowledged the patient's response with empathy

If the patient expressed ANY emotion, the therapist MUST respond
with a brief acknowledgment BEFORE passing.

## Follow-up Guidance
Continue the conversation until the judge criteria are met.
