# Step 1 — Welcome Back

## Prompt Task
Welcome the patient back.

1. "Welcome back, I'm glad you're here."
2. Acknowledge that coming back takes effort
3. Ask how they're feeling about being here today

2-3 sentences.

## Judge Criteria
pass=true if the patient responded to the greeting in any way.

Examples that PASS:
- "I'm doing better this week" → pass
- "I'm okay" → pass
- "I didn't want to come" → pass (reluctance is fine at this point)

This is just a greeting — pass on the patient's FIRST response.

## Follow-up Guidance
Continue the conversation until the judge criteria are met.

## Data to Extract
observation: session_greeting

## Clinical Notes
- Keep this brief — 1 turn
- If patient expresses reluctance, the therapist's greeting message
  itself should be warm enough. Move on to Step 2.
