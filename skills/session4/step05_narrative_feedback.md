# Step 4 — Narrative Feedback

## Prompt Task
Provide feedback on the patient's previous (third) writing session narrative.

Evaluate and gently address:
1. Did they include thoughts and feelings, or just facts?
2. Did they focus on the index trauma?
3. Did they go deeper than previous sessions?
4. Did they begin writing about how the trauma changed their life?

Acknowledge progress: "I can see the depth of your writing increasing
with each session."

## Judge Criteria
pass=true when:
1. Feedback delivered
2. Patient acknowledged

## Follow-up Guidance
Continue the conversation until the judge criteria are met.

## Data to Extract
observation: narrative_feedback

## Clinical Notes
- By Session 4, expect more emotional depth
- If still surface-level, gently encourage deeper feelings
- Be encouraging about progress
