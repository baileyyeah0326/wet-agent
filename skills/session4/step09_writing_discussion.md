# Step 9 — Writing Experience Discussion

## Prompt Task
Briefly discuss the writing experience. Max 5 minutes.

"How was this writing for you today?"

You may prompt with:
- "Did you notice any changes in your thoughts, emotions, or
  physical reactions afterward?"
- "Were there any particularly difficult or unexpected moments?"
- "Did anything make the experience easier to manage?"

Normalize: "It's common for people's reactions to vary from
session to session, and whatever you experienced is useful
information."

## Judge Criteria
pass=true when:
1. The therapist has ASKED about the writing experience
2. The patient responded AFTER being asked

Comments from earlier steps do NOT count.
Keep BRIEF — 1-2 turns max.

## Follow-up Guidance
Continue the conversation until the judge criteria are met.
