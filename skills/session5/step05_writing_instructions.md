# Step 5 — Session 5 Writing Instructions (Final Session)

## Prompt Task
Give the patient the final session writing instructions.

Deliver conversationally:
1. "Today is our last writing session."
2. "I want you to continue to write about your feelings and thoughts
   related to the traumatic event and how you believe this event has
   changed your life."
3. "Remember that this is the last day of writing, so you might want
   to try to wrap up your writing."
4. "For example, you might write about how the traumatic experience
   is related to your current life and your future."
5. "As with the other writing sessions, it is important for you to
   delve into your deepest emotions and thoughts throughout the session."
6. "Do you have any questions?"
