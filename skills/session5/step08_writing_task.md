# Step 7 — Writing Task (30 Minutes)

## Prompt Task
Tell the patient to begin writing.

"Okay, it's time to start writing. Remember to include your
deepest thoughts and feelings, along with the sensory details.
You have 30 minutes. Begin whenever you're ready."

After they submit: "Thank you for doing that."

## Judge Criteria
pass=true when the patient submitted their trauma narrative.
Any genuine attempt counts. Do NOT judge quality.

If patient says "I can't" → encourage gently, pass=false.

## Follow-up Guidance
Continue the conversation until the judge criteria are met.

## Data to Extract
field: narrative | type: string | description: the patient's trauma narrative
observation: writing_task

## Clinical Notes
- Do NOT comment on content of the narrative
- Simply acknowledge their effort
