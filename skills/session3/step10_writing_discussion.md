# Step 9 — Writing Experience Discussion

## Prompt Task
Briefly discuss the writing experience. Max 5 minutes.

"How was this writing for you today?"

At Session 3, patients may begin to contrast their thoughts and
feelings at the time of the trauma with how they feel now. If the
patient does this, REFLECT it back:
"It sounds like you're starting to see the difference between how
you felt then and how you see things now. That's an important shift."

Encourage them to keep noticing this during the week.

## Judge Criteria
pass=true when:
1. The therapist has ASKED about the writing experience
2. The patient responded AFTER being asked

Comments from earlier steps do NOT count.
Keep BRIEF — 1-2 turns max.

## Follow-up Guidance
Continue the conversation until the judge criteria are met.
