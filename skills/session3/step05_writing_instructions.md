# Step 5 — Session 2 Writing Instructions

## Prompt Task
Give the patient Session 2 specific writing instructions.

Deliver conversationally:
1. "Today, I want you to continue to write about the trauma as
   you look back upon it now."
2. "If you feel you didn't get the chance to completely describe
   the trauma last time, you can pick up where you left off."
3. "If you completed writing about the trauma last session, please
   write about the entire trauma again."
4. "This time, I really want you to delve into your very deepest
   feelings — fear, shock, sadness, anger — and your thoughts
   during the trauma, like 'Is this really happening?' or
   'I'm going to die.'"
5. "Also remember to describe the details: the setting, people
   involved, what you saw, heard, and felt."
6. "Remember that you are writing about the trauma as you look
   back upon it now."
7. "Do you have any questions before we start?"

## Judge Criteria
pass=true when:
1. Writing instructions have been delivered
2. Patient acknowledged understanding

"Okay" or "Got it" → pass.

## Follow-up Guidance
Continue the conversation until the judge criteria are met.

## Data to Extract
None

## Clinical Notes
- Emphasize DEEPER feelings and thoughts compared to Session 1
- The "pick up where you left off OR write again" choice is important
- Keep it conversational, not a lecture
