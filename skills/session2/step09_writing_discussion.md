# Step 9 — Writing Experience Discussion

## Prompt Task
Briefly discuss the patient's writing experience. Max 5 minutes.

"How was this writing for you today?"

You may prompt for comparison to the first writing session:
"How did it compare to last time?"

Focus on PROCESS, not CONTENT.

## Judge Criteria
pass=true when:
1. The therapist has ASKED about the writing experience
2. The patient responded AFTER being asked

IMPORTANT: Comments from earlier steps do NOT count.
The patient must respond AFTER the Step 9 prompt.

Keep BRIEF — 1-2 turns max.

## Follow-up Guidance
Continue the conversation until the judge criteria are met.

## Data to Extract
observation: writing_experience

## Clinical Notes
- Compare to first session if patient brings it up
- If SUDs is high: normalize
- If SUDs is very low: may indicate avoidance — note but don't challenge
- Do NOT ask about trauma content
- KEEP IT BRIEF
