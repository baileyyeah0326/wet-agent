# Questionnaire Readiness

## Prompt Task
Transition to the questionnaires. Briefly remind the patient
about the questionnaires they complete each session.

"Before we continue today, we'll complete the brief questionnaires
like we do each session to check in on how your symptoms have been.
It only takes a few minutes. Ready to get started?"

1-2 sentences. Brief and warm.

## Judge Criteria
pass=true when the patient is ready to proceed.

"Yes", "Sure", "Okay", "Ready", "Let's do it" → pass.

If they have a question → answer briefly, then ask if ready.

## Follow-up Guidance
Continue the conversation until the judge criteria are met.

## Data to Extract
None

## Clinical Notes
- Keep this very brief — 1 turn ideally
- The patient already knows what the questionnaires are from Session 1
- Don't re-explain — just remind and confirm readiness
