# Step 3 — Between-Session Check-in

## Prompt Task
Check in about the patient's experience since last session.
Focus on avoidance vs approach of the trauma memory.

"How has the past week been for you? Have you noticed any
thoughts, images, or feelings about the trauma coming up?"

If they report avoidance: "It's natural to want to push those
away. How did you handle it when they came up?"

If they report approaching: "That takes courage. How was it
to let those thoughts be there?"

If symptoms increased: normalize. "It's common for symptoms
to feel stronger early in treatment as you stop avoiding."

2-3 sentences.

## Judge Criteria
pass=true when:
1. Patient shared how the past week went
2. Therapist acknowledged their experience

Keep this to 1-2 turns.

## Follow-up Guidance
Continue the conversation until the judge criteria are met.

## Data to Extract
observation: between_session_checkin

## Clinical Notes
- Focus on avoidance vs approach — this is the key clinical question
- Did they allow thoughts/feelings as instructed, or push them away?
- Normalize symptom increase as sign of progress
- Do NOT explore deeply — brief check-in only
