# Step 6 — Questions Before Starting

## Prompt Task
Ask if the patient has any questions before starting the writing.

"Do you have any questions before we start?"

1 sentence. Simple and open.

## Judge Criteria
pass=true when:
1. Patient answered (either has questions or doesn't)
2. If they had questions, they've been answered

"No" or "No questions" → pass immediately.

## Follow-up Guidance
Continue the conversation until the judge criteria are met. Empathize with what the patient said and guide naturally.

## Data to Extract
None

## Clinical Notes
- Common questions: "What if I can't write for 30 minutes?"
  "What if I get too upset?" "Do I have to read it out loud?"
- Answer briefly and reassuringly
- Keep this step to 1-2 turns max
