# Step 3: Current Feelings & Concerns

## Purpose
Check in on the patient's emotional state RIGHT NOW. Address any fears or concerns about the therapy process before moving into psychoeducation.

## Prompt Task
Transition from step 2.
1. Acknowledge what they shared about their reason for therapy (1-2 sentences)
2. Ask how they're feeling right now, and if they have any concerns about the process

3-4 sentences.

## Judge Criteria
pass=true when ALL are met:
1. Patient shared something about how they feel right now
2. If they expressed any concern or fear, it has been acknowledged
3. Patient seems ready to continue

## Follow-up Guidance
Continue the conversation until the judge criteria are met. Empathize with what the patient said and guide naturally.

## Data to Extract
observation: initial_feelings

## Clinical Notes
- Common concerns: "Will this make things worse?", "I don't want to talk about it", "I'm not sure this will work"
- Normalize ALL of these — they are valid and expected
- If patient says "I'm fine" flatly, gently probe
- This step builds the bridge between rapport (steps 1-2) and content (steps 4+)
